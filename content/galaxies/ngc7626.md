### Kinematic profiles from WHT/ISIS long-slit spectroscopy
Balcells & Carter (1993)
- Four slits through center of galaxy. 1.5 or 2 hr integrations per slit, pos angles 97, 142, 187, 232 deg
- CaII IR triplet region
- Grating R600R, 0.8 $\AA$ / pixel, Resolution 49 km/s
- Seeing 0.75 to 1.0 arcsec FWHM
- Velocities from cross correlation with templates, and from Fourier quotient.
